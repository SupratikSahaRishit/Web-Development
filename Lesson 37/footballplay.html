Process for playing Football

Playing soccer involves two 45-minute halves starting with a kickoff, with the goal of scoring by placing the ball into the opponent's net using any body part except hands/arms (unless you are the goalkeeper).


Key elements include mastering ball control (passing, dribbling, shooting) with feet, chest, or head, and applying tactical awareness to move the ball.

Equipments needed for playing football

  1. Football
  2. Boots
  3. Goalkeeping gloves
  4. Good Anklets
  5. Branded socks
