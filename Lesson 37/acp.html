Football Information

Football


Football (or soccer) is a global team sport where two teams of 11 players try to score by getting a ball into the opposing goal, mainly using their feet, with the goalkeeper being the exception for hand use in their penalty area; it's the world's most popular sport, codified in England in the 19th century, and its premier event is the FIFA World Cup. Other "football" codes, like American football, involve carrying the ball and have different rules, but the core idea is scoring by moving a ball past opponents.

Steps of Football


To play football (soccer), learn basic skills like dribbling, passing, shooting, and controlling the ball, understand simple rules (no hands, score in goal), and practice consistently through drills and playing with others to build fitness and game sense, focusing on body positioning and using different parts of your foot.
