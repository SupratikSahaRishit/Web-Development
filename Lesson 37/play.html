Process for playing Cricket

To play cricket, set up a pitch with wickets at each end, divide into two teams of 11, and have one team bat while the other bowls and fields; batsmen score runs by hitting the ball and running between wickets or hitting boundaries, while the fielding team tries to get batsmen "out" (e.g., by hitting wickets, catching the ball) before the batting team completes their innings, with the team scoring the most runs winning.
