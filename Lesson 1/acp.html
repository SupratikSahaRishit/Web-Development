The Solar System is the Sun and everything bound by its gravity, including eight planets, their moons, dwarf planets,asteroids, and comets .
It formed from a collapsing nebula about 4.6 billion years ago, with the Sun, a central star, containing most of the system's mass and energy through nuclear fusion.
The planets, from innermost to outermost .
Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune, with the inner four being rocky and the outer four being gas giants .
